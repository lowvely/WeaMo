🌤️ About Us

Who We Are

WeaMo is a research project developed by Computer Engineering students from the University of Mindanao - Tagum College. It is an autonomous weather monitoring and prediction system that provides real-time, localized weather forecasts through environmental sensors and AI-powered cloud classification.

Our Mission

To empower farmers and communities with accurate, real-time, and localized weather information that helps in making better agricultural and daily decisions. By combining modern sensors, machine learning, and cloud image analysis, WeaMo bridges the gap between broad national forecasts and the specific needs of local areas.

What We Do

  • ✔️ Real-time monitoring of temperature, humidity, pressure, and wind speed
  • ✔️ Cloud classification using Artificial Intelligence for short-term prediction
  • ✔️ Solar-powered, sustainable hardware design
  • ✔️ Mobile and web applications for easy access to hourly updates and forecast history
